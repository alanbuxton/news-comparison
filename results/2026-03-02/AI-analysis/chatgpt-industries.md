* Product launches (recycled resin launches)
* Strategy shifts in media distribution

**3. Published dates**
Dates in the dataset appear consistent with article timelines (within expected timezone tolerance).

---

### Weaknesses

* PR Newswire and GlobeNewswire appear frequently.

* Plastics News likely requires subscription.

**3. Some broader “industry adjacent” hits**
Occasionally articles touch the geography but are less directly tied to the specific industry-location combination.

---

### Verdict on Exa

✔ Very strong for strategic preparation
✔ Good signal-to-noise balance
✔ Delivers meaningful trends, not catalog content

**Recommended as a primary source.**
